To Do List

Create a Todo item with its due date and tag (if applicable)!

  • Once you complete it, click "Done" button to remove.
  • The "# days left" column shows how many days you have left to complete the task from the creation date on this page.
    • ex) Created "task1" on 9/7 and its due date is 9/9. The # days left column would show "2 day(s)".
